About me

I am a last-year Ph.D. Candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of California Riverside, advised by Dr. Hyoseung Kim and Dr. Cong Liu. I have received M.S. Computer Engineering in University of California Riverside, advised by Dr. Hung-Wei Tseng, and B.S. Cognitive Science with Specialization of Human-Computer Interaction in University of California San Diego. I have also worked as an EDG intern in MathWorks™ GPU coder team and senior full-stack software developing engineer in Verizon™ GNT group.

My research interests is:

• Realtime AI Systems: Researching efficient AI algorithms and system frameworks for time-sensitive AI workloads including generative recommender systems, wearable AI, and edge AI systems.
• Real-time eXtended Reality (XR): Designing efficient and practical XR system framework to enable fast-growing AI glasses, VR/AR and XR teleoperation industries.
