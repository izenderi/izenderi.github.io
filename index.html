About me

I am currently pursuing Ph.D. Electrical and Computer Engineering in University of California Riverside, advised by Dr. Hyoseung Kim and Dr. Cong Liu. I have received M.S. Computer Engineering in University of California Riverside, advised by Dr. Hung-Wei Tseng, and B.S. Cognitive Science with Specialization of Human-Computer Interaction in University of California San Diego. I have also worked as a senior full-stack software developing engineer in Verizon™ GNT group.

Current research interests are:

• Real-time eXtended Reality: Designing efficient and practical XR system framework to enable fast-growing XR deployment.
• Machine Learning on Real-Time Embedded System: Supporting high performance machine learning workloads on real-time embedded system like battery-less devices.
